Minimum version to build first 
If you want the leanest useful version, start with just these 6 event types: 
? signal_received 
? entry_decision 
? order_result 
? position_opened 
? position_action trade_closed 

That alone would give you about 80% of the value. 
One rule to follow 
For every signal, you should be able to answer: 
1. what came in 
2. what the bot decided 
3. why it decided that 
4. what price it actually got 
5. how the trade was managed 
6. how it ended 
If you can answer those six from your logs, your system becomes much easier to improve.
